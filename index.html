About Me

My coding adventures began with COBOL, thanks to the big Y2K scare. I was hired at Travelers Insurance Company, trained for 8 weeks in COBOL, and then placed in a job fixing date fields to accept a 4 digit number. After 3 years, I was ready to learn more and was hired at Liberty Mutual, again into a training program. This time I was exposed to both front-end and back-end technology. Once my training program was completed, I was placed in a position where I was mostly working with java. I worked in this group for 4 years until my daughter was born. I have dedicated my time since then to raising my kids, but I am now ready to get my head back in some code!
