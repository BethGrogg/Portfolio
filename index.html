About Me

I am a Web Developer with a background in OutSystems, Java, C#, XML, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My first experience with these programs was 25 years ago while I was in a training program at Liberty Mutual. Here I was able to build a system from scratch while spearheading the project and working with other individuals to reach a common goal of creating a positive interactive experience.

I took a break from coding to work within my children’s school system, allowing me to be on the same schedule they were on. Now that they are older, I have continued my previous career in programming. To kickstart this process, I completed the University of New Hampshire’s Full Stack Bootcamp where I expanded my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and SQL as well as learned MongoDB, Node, Express, and React.

As a web developer, there is always something to learn and new problems to solve. My past experience in older languages and systems gives me a unique perspective on web development as I have a strong understanding of the basics of coding logic as there were fewer tools available to aid in coding and debugging.

I believe one of my strengths as a developer is that I think of myself as providing a service to users. Therefore, I want to really understand their needs in order to develop a solution that will meet that need. I also believe in very thorough testing before sending code for user testing. As I am providing a service to the user, I want to give them my best work.
