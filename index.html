About Me

My coding adventures began with COBOL, thanks to the big Y2K scare. I was hired at Travelers Insurance Company, trained for 8 weeks in COBOL, and then placed in a job maintaining a mainframe system. After 3 years, I was ready to learn more and was hired into a training program at Liberty Mutual. This time I was exposed to both front-end and back-end technology. Once my training program was completed, I was placed as a java programmer. I worked in this group for 4 years until my daughter was born. I have dedicated my time since then to raising my kids and working in our school district, but I am now ready to get my head back in some code!
